District of Columbia Municipal Regulations
 
5599
DEFINITIONS 
5599.1
The following terms and phrases shall apply only to Chapter 55 of Title 14 
DCMR:
Initial  PHA  -  the  Public  Housing  Authority  in  the  jurisdiction  where  the 
participant Family currently leases a unit and wishes to move from.
Receiving PHA - the Public Housing Authority in the jurisdiction in which the 
participant Family wishes to lease a rental unit.
SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 59 DCR 7926 (June 29, 2012).
