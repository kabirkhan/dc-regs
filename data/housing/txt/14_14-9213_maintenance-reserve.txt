District of Columbia Municipal Regulations
 
9213     
MAINTENANCE RESERVE
9213.1
The Family shall establish and maintain a maintenance reserve after the family 
has purchased a home for as long as they receive voucher subsidy assistance to 
assist in them in making monthly mortgage payments or for home maintenance 
and repairs. This reserve is to be kept in a bank or credit union of the Family’s 
choice. 
9213.2 
The required maintenance reserve shall be fifty dollars ($50). Participants can 
elect to put more than the established initial reserve amount in their account at 
their discretion. The family must provide evidence of this minimum monthly 
reserve to the HOAP Coordinator as part of their HOAP family obligations at 
periodic recertification or interim.
9213.3
Purchasers who fail to establish a maintenance reserve account, which shall be 
verified periodically, by the HOAP Coordinator, shall be required to attend 
individual counseling sessions.
