District of Columbia Municipal Regulations
 
9115  
ASSETS
9115.1
Households shall not be required to report the value or income from assets that are 
valued at less than $25,000, but shall be required to certify that their combined 
asset value is under $25,000.  This income shall not be included in the income 
calculated to determine TTP.  If a household has assets that are $25,000 or more, 
they shall be required to report this information, and the income from assets shall 
be calculated. 
SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 61 DCR 9596 (September 19, 2014).
