District of Columbia Municipal Regulations
 
9108      
OVERVIEW OF RENT AND SUBSIDY CALCULATIONS
9108.1
In order to calculate family rent to owner for the Rent Reform Demonstration, 
DCHA shall calculate annual income and the TTP pursuant to Section 9107, but 
shall require the family rent to owner to be at least 28% of gross income less any 
applicable utility allowance, or the minimum rent of $75, whichever amount is 
greater.  
9108.2
DCHA has established a hardship policy that shall provide relief to families 
experiencing hardship in paying their rent.  The hardship policy is described in 
Section 9118.
SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 61 DCR 9596 (September 19, 2014); as amended by Final Rulemaking 
published at 61 DCR 12037 (November 21, 2014).
