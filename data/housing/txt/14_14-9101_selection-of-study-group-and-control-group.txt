District of Columbia Municipal Regulations
 
9101
SELECTION OF STUDY GROUP AND CONTROL GROUP 
9101.1
Program participants shall be selected from among current HCVP participant 
families, using a random assignment methodology.  Households shall be selected 
for either the study or control groups prior to their scheduled recertification 
interview.
9101.2
The following households shall be excluded from the study:
(a)
Elderly as defined in this chapter 
(b)
Disabled
(c)
Project Based Voucher
(d)
VASH (Veteran’s Administration Supportive Vouchers) Voucher 
(e)
Enhanced Voucher 
(f)
Moderate Rehabilitation/Single Room Occupancy (SRO) 
(g)
Family Self-Sufficiency (FSS) program participants
(h)
Households exercising the portability option 
(i)
Households who end participation in the program (either voluntarily or 
involuntarily)
(j)
Mixed households of eligible and non-eligible household members
(k)
Families currently receiving a child care deduction.
SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 61 DCR 9596 (September 19, 2014); as amended by Final Rulemaking 
published at 61 DCR 12037 (November 21, 2014).
