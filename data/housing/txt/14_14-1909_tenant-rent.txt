1909
TENANT RENT
1909.1
Tenant Rent shall equal the Contract Rent less the Tenant Assistance Payment, when all utilities 
(except telephone) and other essential services are supplied by the housing provider.
1909.2
When some or all utilities (except telephone) and other essential housing services are not 
supplied by the housing provider and are not included in the Contract Rent, the Tenant Rent shall 
be the Gross Rent less the Tenant Assistance Payment and the Utility Allowance. Tenant Rent 
shall not be less than zero (0) dollars.
SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 33 DCR 4396, 4418 (July 25, 1986).
