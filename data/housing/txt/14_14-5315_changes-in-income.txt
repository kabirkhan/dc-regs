District of Columbia Municipal Regulations
 
5315
CHANGES IN INCOME
5315.1 
Families shall not be required to report any increase in household income between 
periodic or interim recertifications. Any increase in income shall only be included 
in  the  determination  of  annual  household  income  at  the  next  periodic 
recertification.
5315.2 
If the Family has household income and adds an adult Family member with a 
source of income, DCHA shall only include the new adult member income, as 
applicable, in the determination of annual household income at the next periodic 
recertification.
5315.3
If a zero income family adds an adult member with a source of income, DCHA 
shall include the new income in the determination of annual household income 
effective the first of the month following the DCHA approval of the new adult 
member.
