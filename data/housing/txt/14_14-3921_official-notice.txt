District of Columbia Municipal Regulations
 
3921 
OFFICIAL NOTICE
3921.1
During the disposition of a petition or complaint, the Rent Administrator, on his 
or her own motion or on the motion of a party, may take official notice of the 
following:
(a)
Matters of common knowledge;
(b)
Any information contained in the record of the Rental Accommodations 
Division; or
(c)
Any information contained in the records of any federal or District agency, 
board or commission; provided, that all parties have been given notice of 
the Rent Administrator’s intention to do so and have been given an 
opportunity to show the contrary.
3921.2
Official notice taken of any fact shall satisfy a party’s burden of proving that fact.
3921.3
If the Rent Administrator takes official notice of any matter provided in § 3921.1, 
all parties are entitled to be informed in writing of the fact found by the Rent 
Administrator, and to be provided a period of no less than ten (10) days to contest 
the proposed findings of fact before a final decision is issued.
3921.4
Any registration files or other public documents of which the Rent Administrator 
takes official notice shall be entered into the official record of the proceeding in 
accordance with § 3919.1(e).
SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 68 DCR 012634 (December 3, 2021).
