9223
WAIVER
The Executive Director of DCHA may waive any provision of the governing regulations under 
this Chapter 92 that is not otherwise restricted in law, provided such waiver is in writing and sets 
forth the reasons why the waiver is in the best interests of the program. 
SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 5767 (June 21, 2002); as amended 
by Final Rulemaking published at 52 DCR 6180 (July 1, 2005).
