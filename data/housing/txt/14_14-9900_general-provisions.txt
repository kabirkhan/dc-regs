District of Columbia Municipal Regulations
 
9900  
GENERAL PROVISIONS
9900.1
The provisions of this chapter are issued pursuant to the authority under the Short-
Term Rental Regulation Act of 2018, effective April 25, 2019 (D.C. Law 
22-307; 
D.C. Official Code § 30-201.01  et seq.) and Mayor’s Order 2020-104, 
dated October 13, 2020.
9900.2
The provisions of this chapter shall be applicable to all short-term rentals as 
defined by this chapter.
SOURCE: Final Rulemaking 68 DCR 012598 (December 3, 2021).
