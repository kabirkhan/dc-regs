District of Columbia Municipal Regulations
 
2222
SALE BY HEIRS 
2222.1
If an Inclusionary Unit Owner dies, at least one (1) heir, legatee, or other person 
taking title to the Inclusionary Unit by will or by operation of law shall occupy 
the Inclusionary Unit if the Household of such person meets the requirements of 
these regulations. If the Household of such person does not meet the requirements 
of  these  regulations,  such  person  shall  provide  DHCD  with  a  Notice  of 
Availability in accordance with ยง 2206.
