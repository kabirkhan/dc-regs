District of Columbia Municipal Regulations
 
5763
ANNUAL INSPECTION
5763.1
Each occupied unit shall be inspected annually by the Project Owner. A written 
notice of inspection shall be given to the tenant at least two (2) days in advance.
5763.2
Tenant-caused damage discovered during this inspection shall be assessed to the 
tenant after completion of the repairs in accordance with ยง 5760 of this chapter
