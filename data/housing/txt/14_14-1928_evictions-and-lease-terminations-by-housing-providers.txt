1928
EVICTIONS AND LEASE TERMINATIONS BY HOUSING 
PROVIDERS
1928.1
Housing providers shall terminate leases with tenants in accordance with lease terms and District 
law.
1928.2
A tenant evicted through judicial means for non-payment, or violation of the lease or District 
Tenant-Landlord law, shall not be eligible for assistance after the date of eviction.
1928.3
A tenant who shall move, for any other reason, including lease non-renewal, non-judicial 
eviction, or owner non-renewal of the Contract, shall be eligible for continued assistance in a new 
unit as provided in ยง 1929.
