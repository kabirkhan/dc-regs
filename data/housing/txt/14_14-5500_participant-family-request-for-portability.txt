District of Columbia Municipal Regulations
 
5500
PARTICIPANT FAMILY REQUEST FOR PORTABILITY 
5500.1
When a Family wishes to move under portability, the Family shall inform DCHA 
in writing of the area to which the Family wishes to move. DCHA shall assist the 
Family in contacting the PHA where they wish to port (the Receiving PHA) in 
writing, if the Family hasn’t already done so. 
5500.2
DCHA shall contact the Receiving PHA and request the following information:
(a)
Contact person for Receiving PHA;
(b)
Phone number;
(c)
Fax number;
(d)
Physical address and mailing address for Receiving PHA; and
(e)
Any  specific  procedures  the  Family  shall  be  made  aware  of  for 
appointments and voucher issuance.
SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 59 DCR 7926 (June 29, 2012).
