District of Columbia Municipal Regulations
 
3929 
RENT ADMINISTRATOR PROCEDURES GENERALLY
3929.1
When these rules are silent on a procedural issue before the Rent Administrator, 
issues must be decided by using as guidance the current rules of civil procedure 
published and followed by the Superior Court of the District of Columbia.
SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 68 DCR 012634 (December 3, 2021).
