District of Columbia Municipal Regulations
 
5711
NOTIFICATION AND ATTENDANCE
5711.1
The RAD Program shall notify Families in writing, by first class mail or hand 
delivery, of their eligibility for assistance at the time that they are invited to attend 
a briefing. The notice shall identify who is required to attend the briefing, as well 
as the date and time of the scheduled briefing.
SOURCE:  Final Rulemaking at 64 DCR 12956 (December 22, 2017); as amended by Final Rulemaking published 
at 66 DCR 6831 (June 7, 2019).
