District of Columbia Municipal Regulations
 
5407 
ASSETS
5407.1 
DCHA, under its MTW authority, established Initiative 3.7.08 entitled “Rent 
Simplification  and  Collection”,  which  simplified  calculation  of  assets  for 
purposes  of  determining  income.  The  following  provision  shall  apply  to 
calculating assets as imputed income:
(a) 
DCHA shall not require documentation of assets during the periodic 
recertification process; and
(b) 
DCHA shall not consider imputed income from assets in the calculation of 
total annual income.
SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 59 DCR 7819 (June 29, 2012); as amended by Final Rulemaking 
published at 63 DCR 13165 (November 18, 2016).
