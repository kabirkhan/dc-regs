8403
REGULAR PERIODIC RECERTIFICATION
8403.1
Regular recertifications are conducted of family composition, income, allowances and deductions 
by DCHA on a biennial basis or such other basis as may be announced in the District of 
Columbia Municipal Register from time to time.
8403.2
Income limits are not used as a test for continued eligibility at recertification.
SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 54 DCR 9366 (September 28, 2007).
