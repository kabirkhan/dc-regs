9201
PURPOSES
9201.1
The purposes of the HCV/HOAP are to:
(a)
Provide safe, adequate and affordable housing;
(b)
De-concentrate areas of geographic poverty;
(c)
Encourage upward mobility;
(d)
Foster economic self sufficiency; and
(e)
Increase home ownership, while minimizing mortgage default risk.
SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 5768, 5770 (June 21, 2002); as 
amended by Final Rulemaking published at 52 DCR 6180 (July 1, 2005).
