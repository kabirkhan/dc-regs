REFUNDS: A refund of plumbing fee shall be made as follows:
(a)
When no work has been done under authority of permit the fee in 
excess of the cost of inspection to verify no work having been done, 
based  on  $13.00  per  inspector  hour,  the  cost  of  any  engineering 
examination time previously devoted to approval of plans, based on 
$20.00 per hour, plus $19.00 administrative costs of "issuance and 
refund", shall be refunded …
19.00
(b)
When work authorized by permit has been only partially done and when 
the District is satisfied that no more work will be done under the permit, 
the fee in excess of the cost of any Engineering plans examination based 
on $1300 per hour, plus $1900 administrative costs of “issuance and 
refund” shall be refunded …………………………
19.00
(c)
Provided: That request for refund shall be made within six (6) months 
from date of issuance and the permit and receipt are returned to the 
Permit Branch
