District of Columbia Municipal Regulations
 
5403
FAMILIES WHO REPORT NO INCOME
5403.1 
During any periodic or interim recertification where a Family reports they earn no 
income, a worksheet shall be utilized to determine potential undisclosed sources 
of  income.  Families  with  no  income  shall  be  subject  to  periodic  interim 
recertification as further explained in ยง 5604.
