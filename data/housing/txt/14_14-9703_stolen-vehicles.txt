9703
STOLEN VEHICLES
9703.1
If DCHA determines that a vehicle is stolen, whether on DCHA Property or Public Property, the 
DCHA's Office of Public Safety shall report the vehicle stolen on the requisite Metropolitan 
Police Department report form or DCHA staff shall notify the Metropolitan Police Department of 
the stolen vehicle.
SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 51 DCR 2112, 2113 (February 27, 2004).
