District of Columbia Municipal Regulations
 
5300
INCOME  CONSIDERATIONS  AND  DETERMINATION  OF 
TOTAL TENANT PAYMENT
5300.1 
Once a participant is receiving assistance, the following regularly scheduled 
events shall occur:
(a) 
Periodic recertification, in which income is calculated and total tenant 
payment is determined;
(b) 
Interim recertification when necessary; and
(c) 
Housing Quality Standard inspections.
SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 59 DCR 7890 (June 29, 2012); as amended by Final Rulemaking 
published at 63 DCR 9693 (July 22, 2016); as amended by Final Rulemaking published at 63 DCR 13165 
(November 18, 2016).
