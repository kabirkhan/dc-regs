1902
HOUSING QUALITY STANDARDS
1902.1
Units assisted under the program shall be decent, safe and sanitary and in substantial compliance 
with the Housing Code governing the condition of residential premises at the time assistance 
begins and throughout the term of the Tenant Assistance Contract.
1902.2
Units shall be inspected by DCHA prior to tenant admission to determine substantial compliance 
with Housing Code governing the condition of residential premises. Units that do not meet the 
standards described in District regulations may not be approved until corrective action is taken by 
the housing provider as evidenced by a re- inspection of the unit.
SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 33 DCR 4396, 4415 (July 25, 1986); as 
amended by Final Rulemaking published at 36 DCR 4472, 4478 (June 23, 1989).
