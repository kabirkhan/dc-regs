4911 
PRIVACY RIGHTS
4911.1
In conjunction with the HUD Form-50058, applicant and participant families shall 
be required to sign the Federal Privacy Act Statement, which states under what 
conditions HUD will release Family and owner information.
4911.2
DCHA shall only release information to third parties under one or more of the 
following circumstances:
(a) With the signed and written consent of the Head of Household; 
(b) To HUD or other Housing agencies on amounts owed to DCHA for 
claims  paid  and  not  reimbursed  by  a  participant  or  landlord 
regardless of whether there is a repayment agreement; or 
(c) As permitted or required by law.
SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 59 DCR 7856, 7865 (June 29, 2012).
